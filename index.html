How to Add a Plant?

Click the "Add Plant" button in the navigation bar. Fill out the form with your plant's details and upload an image. Click "Add to Collection" to save it.

How Does Storage Work?

This application uses your browser's Local Storage. This means all your plant data is stored directly on your computer. No data is sent to any server. If you clear your browser's cache or data, your plant collection will be erased.

How to Delete a Plant?

Go to the "My Plants" page, click on the plant you want to delete. In the details modal, scroll down and click the "Delete Plant" button.

Privacy Policy

Your data is your own.

Data Collection

Botanica is a static application that runs entirely in your web browser. We do not collect, store, transmit, or process any of your personal data on any external servers. All information you enter, including plant names, notes, and uploaded images, is stored locally in your browser's "Local Storage".

Data Security

Your data's security is dependent on the security of your own computer and web browser. We have no access to it. If you use a shared computer, be aware that other users of that computer may _ be able to access your data.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by (a) deleting individual plants within the app, or (b) clearing your browser's "Local Storage" for this website.
